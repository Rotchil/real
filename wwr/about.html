History

SROF was found in 2000 by the river pionner Rotchil Rdny CHARLES and his 3 brothers. Hence the name SROF: S(Stanley) R(Rotchil) O(Oliver) and F(Fils). by 2005 the company found its permanent home in Saut D'eau. these brothers Turned a fledgling rafting company into what now one of the most respected outfitting companies in Haiti. These brothers were president of SROF for 20 years. they were visionaries entrepreneurs and amang the adventure travel industry's traiblazers. But more importanly, they left a legacy of river conservation and stewardship based on the inspiring idea that spreading the love of wild rivvers is the surest path to their protection, now and for the enjoyment of future generations. Today, their ons are leadind the company. they're dedicated to furthering the company's commitment to the preservation of wild places and creating meaningful outdoor experiences for our guests.
